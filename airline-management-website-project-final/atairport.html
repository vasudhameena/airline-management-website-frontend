DigiYatra

Your face is your new boarding pass. Facial recognition technology lets DigiYatra users skip long queues and move faster through the airport and security. You can use DigiYatra services for domestic flights at major Indian airports, such as Delhi, Bengaluru, Varanasi, Kolkata, Vijayawada, Pune, Hyderabad, and others. Download the app.
